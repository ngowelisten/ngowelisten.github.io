Vision
We Listen was born with a vision where each individual is given mental health support, a safe environment to address their emotions, and easy access to help and therapy procedures when in a crisis. We are working towards providing an environment where mental health is not considered taboo.
Mission

We Listen focuses on creating awareness, breaking the taboo and shame and to lend support to the young people suffering from mental health problems. We aim to create and provide for a virtual space where people can talk about their feelings, emotional pain and distress without any constraints.

Konsciously
We Listen collaborated with Konsciously - a platform that provides its users with a series of interactive and transformational journeys based on the fields of cognitive psychology, philosophy, and spirituality. Through this collaboration, We Listen and Konsciously worked towards achieving the shared aims of helping individuals attain psychological resilience and emotional well-being.
