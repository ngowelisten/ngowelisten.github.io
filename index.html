Vision

We, at NGO ‘We Listen’ (registration process underway), envision a safe space for adolescents and young people, where they are listened to and are addressed about their feelings. Our vision is to create an atmosphere where mental health is not considered a taboo and therapy procedures are easily accessible to all.

Activities at a glance

Current Campaigns

#WeKnow

#WeKnow is a series of online creatives posted on all our social media platforms creating awareness on mental health, the myths associated with them, the issues & problems, their types, causes, and the grounding techniques.

#WeQuote

#WeQuote is a series of weekly online posts in which motivational & inspirational quotes written either by famous personalities or contributed to us by our audience, revolving around mental health, are restated by us.

Upcoming Events

Video Initiative

We are trying to reproduce the journeys of people who had been through some mental health issues in the past or are still making their way through and spread their words through videos to be posted on our online platforms. The motivation behind this initiative is to encourage people to give and seek help, open up, and break the stigma associated with mental health in our society.

#WeCare [May Mental Health Month]

May is observed as Mental Health Awareness Month and We Listen is organizing a full month event consisting of activities engaging our audience with the goal to educate people, fight the stigma and provide support related to mental health and the mental health issues.

Professional Forum

This forum connects the people to professionals or specialists on an online platform where the queries, myths, or doubts regarding mental health and the problems are posted anonymously and are addressed by the professionals providing basic help, education, and solutions to them.

Freelance Projects

We aim to launch freelance projects which will provide a platform for people who are enthusiastic to be a part of the social cause and social work by contributing through various articles, PPTs, research-related work, etc created or done by them to help us to build a strong network with the contributors focusing on innovation, creativity, and productivity.
