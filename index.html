Vision
We envision a safe space for humans, a place where they are heard and reciprocated. We vision to create World where mental health isn’t a taboo or stigmatized.

WeListen Forum
We Listen forum is an online platform that connects you to the professionals on-board with us. On this forum, you can anonymously share your feelings & thoughts or can post your queries & doubts regarding mental health. The forum is active on the social media platform “Facebook”.

TALE
We are reproducing the journeys of people who had been through some mental health issues in the past or are still making their way through and spread their words through videos to be posted on our online platforms. The motivation behind this initiative is to encourage people to give and seek help, open up, and break the stigma associated with mental health in our society.

Myth Busters
Myth Busters
Through MythBusters, NGO- We Listen is working towards breaking the stigma towards mental health and the issues prevalent in the society. Through creatives posted online, we’re busting the common myths around mental health issues and their treatment - an initiative to normalise the therapies and medications associated with mental health.

We Know
We Know
A series of online creatives posted on all our social media platforms creating awareness on mental health, the myths associated with them, the issues & problems, their types, causes, and the grounding techniques. To date, we have covered the mental issues of anxiety and anxiety disorder, focusing on what these issues are about and their grounding techniques.

Collaborations

Konsciously
Konsciously
On 8th August, 2020, NGO- We Listen officially announced a collaboration with Konsciously - a place for consciousness & wisdom. They are designed to help users facing emotional distress to achieve holistic well-being. Through this collaboration, We Listen and Konsciously would be working towards achieving the shared aims of helping individuals attain psychological resilience and emotional well being.
Wysa
Wysa
We collaborated with Wysa, an AI chat that makes you feel heard and supports you in difficult times. Wysa has helped over a million people last year and we too hope to help numerous people through the collaboration.
