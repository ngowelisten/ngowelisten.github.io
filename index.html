Vision

We, at NGO ‘We Listen’ (registration process underway), envision a safe space for adolescents and young people, where they are listened to and are addressed about their feelings. Our vision is to create an atmosphere where mental health is not considered a taboo and therapy procedures are easily accessible to all.

Activities at a glance

Current Campaigns

#WeKnow

#WeKnow is a series of online creatives posted on all our social media platforms creating awareness on mental health, the myths associated with them, the issues & problems, their types, causes, and the grounding techniques.

#WeQuote

#WeQuote is a series of weekly online posts in which motivational & inspirational quotes written either by famous personalities or contributed to us by our audience, revolving around mental health, are restated by us.

Upcoming Events

Video Initiative

We are trying to capture the journey of people who've fought bravely with mental health issues & spread their word through videos. The sole aim being to encourage people to give and seek help, open up, and break the stigma associated with mental health in our society.

#WeCare [May Mental Health Awareness Month]

We Listen organizes events spanning the entire month with the goal to educate people, fight the stigma and provide support related to mental health and the mental health issues.

Professional Forum

A forum connecting the audience to specialists to address their queries, myths, or doubts regarding mental health and the problems through anonymous posts.

Freelance Projects

A platform for people who are enthusiastic to be a part of freelance projects related to social cause and social work by contributing through various articles, PPTs, research-related work, etc thereby building a strong network with the contributors.
