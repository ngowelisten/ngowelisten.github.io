Our Journey

An initiative undertaken by a group of young people with an idea to create awareness, break the stigma associated with mental health problems & lend support to the young people suffering from such issues. It has now become our dream that all those going through a difficult time can be given the necessary help in time & can successfully strive through to the brighter end.
